mtimes

(method of EBSD)

rotating the ebsd data by a certain rotation

overloads the * operator

Syntax 

ebsd = g * ebsd % rotates the EBSD data by orientation g
ebsd = ebsd * v % rotate a vector3d v by EBSD data
